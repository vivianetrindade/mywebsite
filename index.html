Who am I?

This website was made to talk a little about me a Brazilian nurse woman that end up in Sweden in the search of a change in the career path.

My amazing career path.

My background is in health care as a nurse with specialization in surgery nursing, so I have 6 years of experience in the surgery department and sterilization center.

The willing to make difference in people lives in a bigger scale I worked for 4 years in a health insurance company as Senior Contract Analyst.

In search of a new path we came to Stockholm and found a master's degree in connecting health with technology. While doing my master's I have been studying IT because I believe it is the future.

This is a little of my journey that I am very proud of it. I do not have afraid of changes and learned a lot with every one of them.
